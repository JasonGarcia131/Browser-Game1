  • Black JACK-O-LANTER Rules:

  • Start Game:

    1. Click on the tombstone image to start game.
    2. You will be dealt two cards at the beginning of the game.
    3. Both you and the dealer will start with $1,000
    4. Each round will deduct $100 from each player's wallet.
    5. If the sum of the cards value is less than 21, you can choose to click hit or stay
      • Hit: will deal you another card.
      • Stay: will end your turn.

  • How to win a round:

    • You win if:
      • The sum of your cards is equal to 21 or is greater than the sum of the dealers cards.
      • You deal up all five cards on your side and the sum is less than 21.
      • The dealer's card sum is greater than 21

  • How to win the game:

    • You must win enough rounds to get the dealer's wallet to $0.00
      • Each round requires $100 entry to play.
